Building Resilience to Urban Disaster

Trondheim live project

An EiT project @ NTNU January 2015

Introduction

This website contains information about a study done by 19 students from the Norwegian University of Science and Technology during January 2015. The course Experts in Teamwork is mandatory for all master-level students. The topic for the groups work was "Building resilience to Urban Disaster", and it was conducted with the city of Trondheim as the subject of study. We looked at historical events in Trondheim, interviewed several key informants, talked to people on the street and identified possible problems facing Trondheim, and came up with six measures to combat them.
